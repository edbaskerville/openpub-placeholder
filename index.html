The OpenPub Project

The OpenPub Project is an experiment testing future directions in scholarly publishing for ecology, evolutionary biology, and the earth, ocean and environmental sciences. We’re launching a website to host scientific preprints and to enable discussion and review of these new works. We’re throwing in a reputation system to boot, so authors and readers can assess the usefulness of reviews and comments. And it’s all going to be done out in the open.
